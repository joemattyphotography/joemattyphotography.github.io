Bio

Joe Matty is a fine art photographer currently living in Gibsonia, Pennsylvania. He shoots his images using both digital and film, however prefers using film, using formats from 35mm to 4×5 sheet film. He also works in an alternative process known as Mordancage, creating dreamy and unreal images.

Always having loved making pieces at art, Joe knew he wanted to do something artistic but not quite what until he discovered his passion for photography.

Joe believes that everyone can find meaning in art, but not everyone will find the same one.
