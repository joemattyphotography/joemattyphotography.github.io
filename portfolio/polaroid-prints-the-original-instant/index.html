Polaroid Prints – The Original Instant

Shooting instant film is simply something that I just love to shoot. It’s something I find to be in my top two favorite formats to shoot on. Even though they’re not the most sharp looking images, I wanted to put a gallery of them on here together for that exact reason. I like to think that the soft look they have gives a more calm, fun, and ‘homey’ feel to them, Even though some of them may have a darker overall tones.

All images were taken on Fuji FP-100C with a Polaroid 250 Land Camera.
