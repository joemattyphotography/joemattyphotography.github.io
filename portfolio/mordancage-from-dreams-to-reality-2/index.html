Mordançage – From Dreams to Reality

Mordançage is an interesting process, to say the least. Starting with silver gelatin prints and then processing through the Mordançage solution,the emulsion lifts off creating intricate and beautiful veils. However, sometimes the veils aren’t what you always want in the print, and you can have effects such as oxidization adding different moods to the print through color. Or just remove the veil for a completely blank space! Here is a collection of some of my Mordançage processed prints. Be creative and see if you can find a print that speaks to you!
