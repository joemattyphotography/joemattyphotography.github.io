Mordancage – From Dreams to Reality

Mordancage is an interesting process, to say the least. Starting with silver gelatin prints and then processing through the Mordancage solution,

the emulsion lifts off creating intricate and beautiful veils. However, sometimes the veils aren’t what you always want in the print, and you can have effects such as oxidization adding different moods to the print. Here is a collection of some of my Mordancage processed prints. Be creative and see if you can find a print that speaks to you!
